Navigating WHOP Content

Welcome to level 1 (aka WHOP) of LetPhil! This page tells you about the different resources and content available in the LetPhil VIP discord server to help you successfully navigate and complete level 1 of your programming journey.

Keywords

  • discord server = LetPhil VIP
  • discord channel = Labels associated with symbols (like #)
  • discord message = Any message posted by a person in a channel feed

Level 1 Mentorship Channels

When you are in the LetPhil VIP discord server, go down the sidebar until you reach the Lvl 1 Mentorship dropdown menu. Two channels will appear under Lvl 1 Mentorship:

  1. whop-lab
  2. lvl-1-roster

whop-lab

This channel contains mini-projects from past WHOP classes you can view and/or do for practice.

lvl-1-roster

This channel contains the chat feed created for WHOP-related conversations between the WHOP mentor and current WHOP mentees.

Where to Find the Class Schedule

  1. Click on the menu item at the very top of the discord server side panel called Events with a calendar icon next to it.
  2. Scroll through and find all courses listed under the title "Lvl 1 Whop (Web Dev Foundation Basics) Class" (aka WhopLab).
